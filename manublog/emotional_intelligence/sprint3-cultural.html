Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

It's the ability that people have to undertand their and other people emotions and the skill of manage those emotions in positive ways.
This is not an ability that we born with. It's an ability we have to train it to develop.

What is IQ?

Intelligence quotient (IQ), is a measure of a person's reasoning ability.
It is a way to gauge how well someone can use information and logic to solve problems.

What is the difference between EQ and IQ?

The difference between IQ and EQ is that while IQ is the measure of a person's relative intelligence and the ability of use the logic to solve problems.
EQ is the ability to identify and manage your emotions and other people emotions, and have a better approach at the moment of solving a problem, considering other people opinions to slove the problem in the best way possible.

Why is EQ so important?

We can't say that EQ is more important that IQ, bouth of them are parts of our intelligence.
What we can say is as child we have been educated in a way that cares more about developing our IQ that our EQ (school sistem).
So it's important to develop our EQ so we can improve our skill of looking at a problem from different points of view and try to find the best solution.
Developing our EQ will give us the chance to improve our Performance, Leadership skills and happines.
