Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset

How understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people?

Neuroplasticity is the abililty of the brain to adapt to changes in an individual's environment by forming new neural connections over time.
It has been proven that people's brains are constantly changing, this changes take time and work. and understanding this would help us to use it in our own benefit.
This changes will be achieved through lots of practicing and studing.

How might engage with the principals of neuroplasticity for our own benefit?

A way to engage with the principals of neuroplasticity is trying new experiences, to stablish new neural pathways.
This neural pathways are going to be our new "motorways" that connect out neurons, this connection is called synapses.
Once we understand that our brains are in constant changing, and we can control this changes through practice, we can star using neuroplasticityin our own benefit.
