Diference between Margin, Border and Padding

This propertys are tools to define the limites that surrounds a content, the main diference is that each of them have a diferent relation with the content and between themselves.

Margin

It's the property that sets the space taht is outside the content, it is manage trought diferents propertys and values.

Border

It's the property that allows us to surround the content with a "box", and specify the style, width and color of that "box".

Padding

Padding is the property that sets the space that is inside of any defined borders.
